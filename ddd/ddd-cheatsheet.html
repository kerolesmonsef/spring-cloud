Domain-Driven Design · Quick Reference

DDD, mapped to folders

Subject: an e-wallet for stablecoins. The vocabulary of DDD — domain, subdomain, context, aggregate — only earns its keep when you can point at the folder it becomes. This sheet does exactly that.

The four words, and which ones are folders

Word What it is A folder? In the wallet
Domain The whole business problem No — it's the whole app “a digital wallet for stablecoins”
Subdomain A slice of the business: Core / Supporting / Generic No — a classification, a way to think Ledger is Core, Onboarding is Supporting
Bounded Context A boundary where one model is valid Yes — your top-level folder ledger, cashout, identity
Aggregate A cluster guarded by one root Entity Yes — a folder inside a context Account, Cashout
